Artisans can tell their stories to buyers in a beautiful space through stunning products presenting compelling information about its origin, thus cultural appreciation increases, promoting the preservation of traditions and generating better income. The model, created by the consultant offers a program targeting potential markets and specific needs of beneficiaries in different environments and cultures.
