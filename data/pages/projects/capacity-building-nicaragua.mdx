## Aid to Artisans
### Capacity Building Program and Product Development and Design 

Daniela worked with Manos Nicas in Estelí, Nicaragua as part of the Aid to Artisan´s 2017 Business Accelerator. After the assessment the consultant divided the program so the staff can have an extensive Capacity Building training and a Product Development and Design Program as planned.

Manos Nicas created a vision that raised the standards of quality and value added for the artisan ́s community through a chain of stores in touristic locations. Daniela advised regarding the need of product diversification for the stores in order to increase the variety of the current products and its offer through new categories that have great sales potential. The new PD was developed into a catalogue of ideas in different trades.
