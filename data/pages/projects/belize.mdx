### Craft Specialist
#### Local market development through training and product design & development. 
#### Craft Specialist for the project “Making Tourism Benefit Communities Adjacent to Archaeological Sites (MTBCAAS) funded by European Union and Belizean organizations BTB and NICH.

Daniela designed, in coordination with the Tourism Specialist, Judy Karwacki, a program for capacity-building within Belizean organizations and a certification scheme. The concept aim was supporting the development of artisans near archaeological sites by improving the design, quality, and exhibition of their products.

The consultant also encouraged the artisans to develop collective retail strategies suggested by her.
